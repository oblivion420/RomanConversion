台文通訊BONG報: Sin-lâm-hiòng hō͘ Tâi Oa̍t Kau-liû Jia̍t-phut-phut
Kok-li̍p Sêng-kong Tāi-ha̍k Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim & Siā-thoân Hoat-jîn Tâi Qa̍t Bûn-hòa Hia̍p-hōe & Tâi-lô-hōe & Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe téng tan-ūi 佇 2019 nî 11 goe̍h 23 到 24 佇 Sêng-tāi Tâi-bûn 下-koán, kiōng-tông ha̍p-chok pān-裡 tē 4 介 Tâi Oa̍t Jîn-bûn Pí-到 Gián-kiú Kok-chè Gián-thó-hōe, tiûⁿ-面 lāu-jia̍t kún-kún. Ū lâi chū Oa̍t-lâm, 日-pún, Bí-kok, Lō-se-阿, Hân-kok, Sin-加-pho, Pí-lī-sî, Ìn-nî, Thài-kok, Hoat-kok, Eng-kok, Hiong-káng hām Tâi-oân téng 200 gōa ūi ha̍k-chiá, sī le̍k-sú í-lâi 規-無͘ 上-tōa 个 chi̍t-pái.

Chit-pái hōe-gī 个 chū-貯 sī “Kok-chè ha̍p-chok 敆 éng-sio̍k hoat-tián”. 開-bō͘ tián-lé chi̍t-開-sí chhiáⁿ Iû Sò͘-lêng 到-岫 tāi-piáu Seng-tāi 共 lâi chham-加 tián-lé 个 kùi-pin tì-sû mn̄g hó. Chóng-捅-拊 chu-chèng Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 ia̍h tāi-piáu Chhòa Eng-bûn chóng-捅 敆 Tâi-oân 阿-chiu Kau-liû 支-kim-hōe tì-sú hoan-gêng choân tiûⁿ chham-ú 个 kùi-pin. Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú tiong-sim chú-jīm Chiúⁿ Ûi-bûn 到-岫 ia̍h tāi-piáu chú-pān tan-ūi choan-têng iōng Oa̍t-lâm-ōe tì-sû 閣 siāu-介 gián-thó-hōe 个 chong-chí 敆 iû-lâi. Li̍p-hoat-奕ⁿ gōa-kau úi-oân-hōe Ông Tēng-ú úi-oân 嘛 到 tiûⁿ chiok-hok tāi-hōe sun-lī sêng-kong.




Chū 2010 nî pān-裡 tē 1 介 Tâi Oa̍t Jîn-bûn Gián-kiú Kok-chè Gián-thó-hōe 到 焦ⁿ, Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim kò͘-tēng múi 3 冬 pān-裡 chi̍t-pái, 規-無͘ sī lú lâi lú tōa. Tē 1 介 hōe-gī tāi-iok ū 40 gōa 个 ha̍k-chiá chham-加. Chit-pái, 干-焦ⁿ Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-chiá 著 í-keng chhiau-kòe 100 人. 毋-若 án-ne, 佇 細-介 閣 kok gián-kiú Oa̍t-lâm choan-tióng 个 ti-miâ tāi-ha̍k, chhiūⁿ-講 Bí-kok 个 Harvard tāi-ha̍k, Bí-kok Tek-chiu tāi-ha̍k, Lō-se-阿 siā-kho-奕ⁿ, 日-pún Kanda (神田) Gōa-gí Tāi-ha̍k téng téng 个 tan-ūi ia̍h 攏 ū phài 人 chhut-se̍k chham-加. 咱 會-sái khòaⁿ--chhut--lâi, 佇 sin-lâm-hiòng chèng-chhek 个 chhui-捒 chi-hā, 毋-若 chin sêng-kong tài-撞 Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 敆 ha̍k-su̍t 上 个 bi̍t-chhiat kau-liû, 嘛 共 Tâi-oân 捒 chiūⁿ ín-chhōa kok-chè chò Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú 个 tiōng-iàu 支-tē.

Chit-pái hōe-gī 攏-chóng ū an-pâi 3 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng. 頭 chi̍t-tiûⁿ ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 siāu-介 sin-lâm-hiòng chèng-chhek 个 空-khòe 敆 tùi 沬-lâi 个 ǹg-bāng. Tē 2 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ goân-che̍k Lō-se-阿, chit-媽 佇 Bí-kok Tek-chiu Lông-kong Tāi-ha̍k 个 Olga 到-岫 thê-kiong, 內-iông 硩 thàm-thó 1960-1970 nî-tāi Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm chheng-數-liân chèng-chek 个 pí-到. Tē 3 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ 冬-hôa Tāi-ha̍k Khng Pōe-tek 到-岫 thàm-thó Lâu Éng-hok 佇 Oa̍t-lâm, Tâi-oân 敆 Tiong-kok 無-仝-khoán 个 hêng-上. Chit-pái Tâi Oa̍t gián-thó-hōe 个 gī-tê pau-hâm gí-giân, bûn-ha̍k, bûn-hòa, le̍k-sú, siā-hōe, cho̍k-kûn, gē-su̍t, chong-到 téng téng kok-chióng léng-he̍k, 攏-chóng ū 26 个 無-kâng gī-tê 个 tiûⁿ-chhù. Phong-phài 个 內-iông í-keng sêng-kong chhiok-chìn Tâi-oân, Oa̍t-lâm 敆 閣 kok 个 ha̍k-su̍t 敆 bûn-hòa kau-liû.



Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm sui-jiân 無 chèng-sek 个 gōa-kau koan-下, 毋-閣 翼-chè 个 kau-liû soah lú lâi lú jia̍t. Chhiūⁿ 講 Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim tn̂g-kî hām Oa̍t-lâm chē keng ū-miâ 个 tāi-ha̍k ha̍p-chok chò Oa̍t-lâm-gí 到-ha̍k, Oa̍t-lâm-gí jīn-chèng 敆 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú. 𪜶-ūi ū chia 个 kau-liû ha̍p-chok chiah 會-當 chhiok sêng Oa̍t-lâm-gí 到-ha̍k 敆 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú 佇 Tâi-oân hoat-tián 甲 chhiaⁿ-掖ⁿ. 毋-若 án-ne, iû Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim gián-hoat 个 Kok-chè Oa̍t-lâm-gí Jīn-chèng (iVPT) 嘛 得-著 Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-su̍t-介 sêng-jīn, ia̍h lio̍k-sio̍k 得-著 日-pún, Bí-kok, Ò-chiu 个 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú chú-iàu 个 tāi-ha̍k sêng-jīn. Chhiūⁿ-講, 日-pún Osaka (大阪) tāi-ha̍k, Kanda (神田) Gōa-gí Ha̍k-奕ⁿ, Bí-kok Harvard tāi-ha̍k, Ò-chiu kok-li̍p Tāi-ha̍k téng téng. Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim 嘛 kiat-ha̍p Bí-kok, 日-pún, Oa̍t-lâm 敆 Tâi-oân 个 ha̍k-chiá chèng-sek chhut-pán “Oa̍t-lâm-ha̍k Gián-kiú” pòaⁿ-nî khan.

Kīn nî lâi Tâi Oa̍t chi-干 个 hoan-e̍k chhut-pán 嘛 得-著 袂-chió sêng-kó. Chhiūⁿ-講, 2018 nî Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim, Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe hām Oa̍t-lâm Chok-加 Hia̍p-hōe ha̍p-chok hoan-e̍k chhut-pán “Chiàn-hóe Jîn-seng: Oa̍t-lâm Si-jîn 霆 Jūn-bêng Si-soán” (Tâi-bûn 敆 Tiong-bûn pán), 佇 Oa̍t-lâm chhut-pán Tâi-oân pò͘-tē-hì kio̍k-pún “Koat-chiàn Siraya” Oa̍t-bûn-pán. Kin nî Tâi Oa̍t siang hong-面 閣-chài ha̍p-chok chhut-pán “Keng-甲-頭 个 工-san: Oa̍t-lâm Si-soán” (肩上江山) an-pâi sin-chheh hoat-piáu. Oa̍t-lâm Chok-加 Hia̍p-hōe iû hù-hōe-tiúⁿ 霆 Teng-kho chhōa-niá 8 个 人 choan-工 lâi Tâi-oân chham-加 sin-chheh hoat-piáu-hōe. Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe 个 hōe-tiúⁿ 霆 Bêng-jîn, 裡-sū 霆 Chèng-hiông, Ông Gē-bêng téng téng, ia̍h tāi-piáu Tâi-oân bûn-ha̍k-介 chham-ú kau-liû. Hōe--lih ū si-jîn 霆 Kong-kùi téng 人 gîm-si kau-liû, 現-tiûⁿ 氣-薰 lāu-jia̍t kún-kún.

Ū 袂 chió Tâi-oân tho̍k-chiá í-keng kî-thāi chin kú, 開 8 冬 sî-干 chiah hoan-e̍k oân-sêng 个 Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-chiá 霆 Gio̍k-thiam (GS. Trần Ngọc Thêm) 到-岫 个 keng-tián 注-chok “Cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm Bûn-hòa Pún-sek” (探索越南文化本色) ia̍h 佇 chòe-kīn chèng-sek chhut-pán. 霆 Gio̍k-thiam 到-岫 sī Oa̍t-lâm chin chhut-miâ 个 bûn-hòa gián-kiú choan-加, 佇 Lō͘-se-阿 Saint-Peterburg Tāi-ha̍k 提-著 phok-sū ha̍k-ūi, 捌 tam-jīm Oa̍t-lâm 蚵͘-chì-bêng chhī Kok-加 Tāi-ha̍k Siā-hōe Jîn-bûn Tāi-ha̍k Bûn-hòa ha̍k-下 下-chú-jīm, Bûn-hòa Gián-kiú Tiong-sim chú-jīm 閣 kiam Lō-se-阿 Siā-hōe Kho-ha̍k-奕ⁿ bêng-ū 奕ⁿ-sū.

“Cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm Bûn-hòa Pún-sek” 攏-chóng ū 27 chiuⁿ, choân chheh 40 bān jī 較 加, sī 岫ⁿ 欲 se̍k-sāi Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 个 人 一-tēng 愛 tha̍k 个 keng-tián chok-phín. Chit-pún chheh 个 chóng-pian-chip 敆 chóng-到-tèng Chiúⁿ Ûi-bûn 到-岫 chí-chhut, chit-pún chheh 个 摃-hiàn sī 佇 leh 退 Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 个 te̍k-sek 敆 chú-thé-sèng hoat-tián chò ha̍k-su̍t sèng 个 改-soeh. Kòe-氣, 佇 tōa Tiong-kok soa-bûn chú-gī 个 軀-lō͘ 會-貯, 一-ti̍t 攏 共 Oa̍t-lâm chāi-lâi 个 冬-lâm-阿 phìⁿ-siùⁿ chò iá-bân 个 pian-介 tē-khu, thàu-kòe Tiong-hôa bûn-hòa 个 thoân-pò 了-āu, chiah hông khé-bêng 捌 bûn-hòa. 霆 Gio̍k-thiam 到-岫 thê-chhut lūn-chèng hoán-pok án-ne 个 hau-siâu-ōe. Phì-jū 講, Tiong-kok-人 tiāⁿ 硩 講 个 “Liông龍” 上-chá kî-翼 sī ùi 冬-lâm-阿 个 “chôa” 敆 “硞-hî” thiu-上-hòa 了-āu só͘ 瘦-seng 个 khài-liām 敆 tô͘-têng. “承 lông神農” ia̍h-sī goân-chū 冬-lâm-阿 Pah-Oa̍t (百越) bîn-cho̍k 个 lông-挾 bûn-hòa.



Chóng-捅-拊 chu-chèng, Tâi-oân 阿-chiu Kau-liû 支-kim-hōe táng-sū-tiúⁿ Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 chhui-chiàn chit-pún chheh 講: “Chit pún chheh tùi Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 下-捅-sèng 个 biâu-su̍t chheng-chhó 閣 oa̍h-phoat, tùi bûn-hòa lūi-hêng 个 pí-到 hui-siông sin-kî 敆 ū chhòng-kiàn. Góa ū 支-hōe 通 tha̍k tāi-seng chho͘-kó, chhin-chhiūⁿ sī 上 了 chi̍t ha̍k-kî 个 cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa goân-頭 敆 ián-hòa 个 khò-têng, khak-翼 o̍h 著 chin chē.” Chhek-ōe chhut-pán chit-pún chheh 个 Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim hām Tâi Oa̍t Bûn-hòa Hia̍p-hōe 嘛 佇 kū-nî 12 goe̍h 25 敆 12 goe̍h 27 薰-pia̍t 佇 Tâi-lâm 敆 Tâi-pak pān-裡 sin-chheh hoat-piáu-hōe. Lâm Kok Bûn  （TLH 裡-sū）




Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-chiá 佇 Sêng-tāi Tâi-bûn-koán chêng ha̍p-iáⁿ






 Tē 4 介 Tâi Oa̍t jîn-bûn pí-到 gián-kiú kok-chè gián-thó-hōe 開-bō͘ tì-sû.




Kok-li̍p Sêng-kong Tāi-ha̍k Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim & Siā-thoân Hoat-jîn Tâi Qa̍t Bûn-hòa Hia̍p-hōe & Tâi-lô-hōe & Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe téng tan-ūi 佇 2019 nî 11 goe̍h 23 到 24 佇 Sêng-tāi Tâi-bûn 下-koán, kiōng-tông ha̍p-chok pān-裡 tē 4 介 Tâi Oa̍t Jîn-bûn Pí-到 Gián-kiú Kok-chè Gián-thó-hōe, tiûⁿ-面 lāu-jia̍t kún-kún. Ū lâi chū Oa̍t-lâm, 日-pún, Bí-kok, Lō-se-阿, Hân-kok, Sin-加-pho, Pí-lī-sî, Ìn-nî, Thài-kok, Hoat-kok, Eng-kok, Hiong-káng hām Tâi-oân téng 200 gōa ūi ha̍k-chiá, sī le̍k-sú í-lâi 規-無͘ 上-tōa 个 chi̍t-pái.






Chit-pái hōe-gī 个 chū-貯 sī “Kok-chè ha̍p-chok 敆 éng-sio̍k hoat-tián”. 開-bō͘ tián-lé chi̍t-開-sí chhiáⁿ Iû Sò͘-lêng 到-岫 tāi-piáu Seng-tāi 共 lâi chham-加 tián-lé 个 kùi-pin tì-sû mn̄g hó. Chóng-捅-拊 chu-chèng Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 ia̍h tāi-piáu Chhòa Eng-bûn chóng-捅 敆 Tâi-oân 阿-chiu Kau-liû 支-kim-hōe tì-sú hoan-gêng choân tiûⁿ chham-ú 个 kùi-pin. Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú tiong-sim chú-jīm Chiúⁿ Ûi-bûn 到-岫 ia̍h tāi-piáu chú-pān tan-ūi choan-têng iōng Oa̍t-lâm-ōe tì-sû 閣 siāu-介 gián-thó-hōe 个 chong-chí 敆 iû-lâi. Li̍p-hoat-奕ⁿ gōa-kau úi-oân-hōe Ông Tēng-ú úi-oân 嘛 到 tiûⁿ chiok-hok tāi-hōe sun-lī sêng-kong.







Tē 4 介 Tâi Oa̍t jîn-bûn pí-到 gián-kiú kok-chè gián-thó-hōe 開-bō͘.






Îⁿ-toh hōe-gī thó-lūn Oa̍t-lâm, Tâi-oân, 日-pún 敆 Bí-kok 都 pêng ha̍p-chok chhui-捒 kok-chè Oa̍t-lâm-gí jīn-chèng.










Chū 2010 nî pān-裡 tē 1 介 Tâi Oa̍t Jîn-bûn Gián-kiú Kok-chè Gián-thó-hōe 到 焦ⁿ, Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim kò͘-tēng múi 3 冬 pān-裡 chi̍t-pái, 規-無͘ sī lú lâi lú tōa. Tē 1 介 hōe-gī tāi-iok ū 40 gōa 个 ha̍k-chiá chham-加. Chit-pái, 干-焦ⁿ Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-chiá 著 í-keng chhiau-kòe 100 人. 毋-若 án-ne, 佇 細-介 閣 kok gián-kiú Oa̍t-lâm choan-tióng 个 ti-miâ tāi-ha̍k, chhiūⁿ-講 Bí-kok 个 Harvard tāi-ha̍k, Bí-kok Tek-chiu tāi-ha̍k, Lō-se-阿 siā-kho-奕ⁿ, 日-pún Kanda (神田) Gōa-gí Tāi-ha̍k téng téng 个 tan-ūi ia̍h 攏 ū phài 人 chhut-se̍k chham-加. 咱 會-sái khòaⁿ--chhut--lâi, 佇 sin-lâm-hiòng chèng-chhek 个 chhui-捒 chi-hā, 毋-若 chin sêng-kong tài-撞 Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 敆 ha̍k-su̍t 上 个 bi̍t-chhiat kau-liû, 嘛 共 Tâi-oân 捒 chiūⁿ ín-chhōa kok-chè chò Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú 个 tiōng-iàu 支-tē.






Chit-pái hōe-gī 攏-chóng ū an-pâi 3 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng. 頭 chi̍t-tiûⁿ ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 siāu-介 sin-lâm-hiòng chèng-chhek 个 空-khòe 敆 tùi 沬-lâi 个 ǹg-bāng. Tē 2 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ goân-che̍k Lō-se-阿, chit-媽 佇 Bí-kok Tek-chiu Lông-kong Tāi-ha̍k 个 Olga 到-岫 thê-kiong, 內-iông 硩 thàm-thó 1960-1970 nî-tāi Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm chheng-數-liân chèng-chek 个 pí-到. Tē 3 tiûⁿ choan-tê ián-káng 枵-chhiáⁿ 冬-hôa Tāi-ha̍k Khng Pōe-tek 到-岫 thàm-thó Lâu Éng-hok 佇 Oa̍t-lâm, Tâi-oân 敆 Tiong-kok 無-仝-khoán 个 hêng-上. Chit-pái Tâi Oa̍t gián-thó-hōe 个 gī-tê pau-hâm gí-giân, bûn-ha̍k, bûn-hòa, le̍k-sú, siā-hōe, cho̍k-kûn, gē-su̍t, chong-到 téng téng kok-chióng léng-he̍k, 攏-chóng ū 26 个 無-kâng gī-tê 个 tiûⁿ-chhù. Phong-phài 个 內-iông í-keng sêng-kong chhiok-chìn Tâi-oân, Oa̍t-lâm 敆 閣 kok 个 ha̍k-su̍t 敆 bûn-hòa kau-liû.







“Keng-甲-頭 个 工-san: Oa̍t-lâm Si-soán” hoat-piáu-hōe ha̍p-iáⁿ.










Tâi-oân 敆 Oa̍t-lâm sui-jiân 無 chèng-sek 个 gōa-kau koan-下, 毋-閣 翼-chè 个 kau-liû soah lú lâi lú jia̍t. Chhiūⁿ 講 Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim tn̂g-kî hām Oa̍t-lâm chē keng ū-miâ 个 tāi-ha̍k ha̍p-chok chò Oa̍t-lâm-gí 到-ha̍k, Oa̍t-lâm-gí jīn-chèng 敆 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú. 𪜶-ūi ū chia 个 kau-liû ha̍p-chok chiah 會-當 chhiok sêng Oa̍t-lâm-gí 到-ha̍k 敆 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú 佇 Tâi-oân hoat-tián 甲 chhiaⁿ-掖ⁿ. 毋-若 án-ne, iû Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim gián-hoat 个 Kok-chè Oa̍t-lâm-gí Jīn-chèng (iVPT) 嘛 得-著 Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-su̍t-介 sêng-jīn, ia̍h lio̍k-sio̍k 得-著 日-pún, Bí-kok, Ò-chiu 个 Oa̍t-lâm gián-kiú chú-iàu 个 tāi-ha̍k sêng-jīn. Chhiūⁿ-講, 日-pún Osaka (大阪) tāi-ha̍k, Kanda (神田) Gōa-gí Ha̍k-奕ⁿ, Bí-kok Harvard tāi-ha̍k, Ò-chiu kok-li̍p Tāi-ha̍k téng téng. Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim 嘛 kiat-ha̍p Bí-kok, 日-pún, Oa̍t-lâm 敆 Tâi-oân 个 ha̍k-chiá chèng-sek chhut-pán “Oa̍t-lâm-ha̍k Gián-kiú” pòaⁿ-nî khan.






Kīn nî lâi Tâi Oa̍t chi-干 个 hoan-e̍k chhut-pán 嘛 得-著 袂-chió sêng-kó. Chhiūⁿ-講, 2018 nî Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim, Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe hām Oa̍t-lâm Chok-加 Hia̍p-hōe ha̍p-chok hoan-e̍k chhut-pán “Chiàn-hóe Jîn-seng: Oa̍t-lâm Si-jîn 霆 Jūn-bêng Si-soán” (Tâi-bûn 敆 Tiong-bûn pán), 佇 Oa̍t-lâm chhut-pán Tâi-oân pò͘-tē-hì kio̍k-pún “Koat-chiàn Siraya” Oa̍t-bûn-pán. Kin nî Tâi Oa̍t siang hong-面 閣-chài ha̍p-chok chhut-pán “Keng-甲-頭 个 工-san: Oa̍t-lâm Si-soán” (肩上江山) an-pâi sin-chheh hoat-piáu. Oa̍t-lâm Chok-加 Hia̍p-hōe iû hù-hōe-tiúⁿ 霆 Teng-kho chhōa-niá 8 个 人 choan-工 lâi Tâi-oân chham-加 sin-chheh hoat-piáu-hōe. Tâi-bûn Pit-hōe 个 hōe-tiúⁿ 霆 Bêng-jîn, 裡-sū 霆 Chèng-hiông, Ông Gē-bêng téng téng, ia̍h tāi-piáu Tâi-oân bûn-ha̍k-介 chham-ú kau-liû. Hōe--lih ū si-jîn 霆 Kong-kùi téng 人 gîm-si kau-liû, 現-tiûⁿ 氣-薰 lāu-jia̍t kún-kún.







Ū 袂 chió Tâi-oân tho̍k-chiá í-keng kî-thāi chin kú, 開 8 冬 sî-干 chiah hoan-e̍k oân-sêng 个 Oa̍t-lâm ha̍k-chiá 霆 Gio̍k-thiam (GS. Trần Ngọc Thêm) 到-岫 个 keng-tián 注-chok “Cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm Bûn-hòa Pún-sek” (探索越南文化本色) ia̍h 佇 chòe-kīn chèng-sek chhut-pán. 霆 Gio̍k-thiam 到-岫 sī Oa̍t-lâm chin chhut-miâ 个 bûn-hòa gián-kiú choan-加, 佇 Lō͘-se-阿 Saint-Peterburg Tāi-ha̍k 提-著 phok-sū ha̍k-ūi, 捌 tam-jīm Oa̍t-lâm 蚵͘-chì-bêng chhī Kok-加 Tāi-ha̍k Siā-hōe Jîn-bûn Tāi-ha̍k Bûn-hòa ha̍k-下 下-chú-jīm, Bûn-hòa Gián-kiú Tiong-sim chú-jīm 閣 kiam Lō-se-阿 Siā-hōe Kho-ha̍k-奕ⁿ bêng-ū 奕ⁿ-sū.







“Cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm Bûn-hòa Pún-sek” 攏-chóng ū 27 chiuⁿ, choân chheh 40 bān jī 較 加, sī 岫ⁿ 欲 se̍k-sāi Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 个 人 一-tēng 愛 tha̍k 个 keng-tián chok-phín. Chit-pún chheh 个 chóng-pian-chip 敆 chóng-到-tèng Chiúⁿ Ûi-bûn 到-岫 chí-chhut, chit-pún chheh 个 摃-hiàn sī 佇 leh 退 Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 个 te̍k-sek 敆 chú-thé-sèng hoat-tián chò ha̍k-su̍t sèng 个 改-soeh. Kòe-氣, 佇 tōa Tiong-kok soa-bûn chú-gī 个 軀-lō͘ 會-貯, 一-ti̍t 攏 共 Oa̍t-lâm chāi-lâi 个 冬-lâm-阿 phìⁿ-siùⁿ chò iá-bân 个 pian-介 tē-khu, thàu-kòe Tiong-hôa bûn-hòa 个 thoân-pò 了-āu, chiah hông khé-bêng 捌 bûn-hòa. 霆 Gio̍k-thiam 到-岫 thê-chhut lūn-chèng hoán-pok án-ne 个 hau-siâu-ōe. Phì-jū 講, Tiong-kok-人 tiāⁿ 硩 講 个 “Liông龍” 上-chá kî-翼 sī ùi 冬-lâm-阿 个 “chôa” 敆 “硞-hî” thiu-上-hòa 了-āu só͘ 瘦-seng 个 khài-liām 敆 tô͘-têng. “承 lông神農” ia̍h-sī goân-chū 冬-lâm-阿 Pah-Oa̍t (百越) bîn-cho̍k 个 lông-挾 bûn-hòa.







“Cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm Bûn-hòa Pún-sek” sin-chheh hoat-piáu-hōe.











Chóng-捅-拊 chu-chèng, Tâi-oân 阿-chiu Kau-liû 支-kim-hōe táng-sū-tiúⁿ Siau Sin-hông 到-岫 chhui-chiàn chit-pún chheh 講: “Chit pún chheh tùi Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa 下-捅-sèng 个 biâu-su̍t chheng-chhó 閣 oa̍h-phoat, tùi bûn-hòa lūi-hêng 个 pí-到 hui-siông sin-kî 敆 ū chhòng-kiàn. Góa ū 支-hōe 通 tha̍k tāi-seng chho͘-kó, chhin-chhiūⁿ sī 上 了 chi̍t ha̍k-kî 个 cháu-chhōe Oa̍t-lâm bûn-hòa goân-頭 敆 ián-hòa 个 khò-têng, khak-翼 o̍h 著 chin chē.” Chhek-ōe chhut-pán chit-pún chheh 个 Sêng-tāi Oa̍t-lâm Gián-kiú Tiong-sim hām Tâi Oa̍t Bûn-hòa Hia̍p-hōe 嘛 佇 kū-nî 12 goe̍h 25 敆 12 goe̍h 27 薰-pia̍t 佇 Tâi-lâm 敆 Tâi-pak pān-裡 sin-chheh hoat-piáu-hōe.
