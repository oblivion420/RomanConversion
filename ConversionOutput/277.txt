日-pún Náu-iām (Japanese encephalitis), Hiang-káng-跤 (Hong Kong foot, Athlete’s foot) 敆 Tek-kok-phia̍h (German measles) 咱 攏 chai-iáⁿ sī siáⁿ-物. Tān-sī 裡 敢 ū thiaⁿ kòe Tiong-kok Hì-iām?

日-pún Náu-iām sī 佇 1871 nî 日-pún tāi-seng pò-就 个 náu-iām. 爸ⁿ-goân-thé (pathogen) sī chi̍t chióng 爸ⁿ-to̍k, kiò-chò JEV (Japanese Encephalitis virus, 日-pún Náu-iām 爸ⁿ-to̍k). Chit khoán 爸ⁿ-to̍k 會 敢-jiám ti-仔. Tāi-iok 咱 Tâi-oân chhī 佇 ti-牢 个 ti-仔 攏 ū 敢-jiám--著! 閣 lâi chiū sī chi̍t khoán hoe-跤-蠓, seng tèng í-keng ū 敢-jiám 个 ti-仔, 共 hoeh 欶--入-氣, chiah 閣 lâi tèng--人, 共 sin-khu 內-貯 个 爸ⁿ-to̍k chù 入 人 个 sin-thé, hō͘ 人 phòa-爸ⁿ. Chit khoán 蠓-仔 kiò-chò phó͘-thong-蠓, Culex (『家蚊』). JEV 爸ⁿ-to̍k 嘛 sī 1934 nî 日-pún-人 tāi-seng 薰-lî khak-jīn--个.

若-sī Tek-kok-phia̍h, 1740 nî Tek-kok 伊-seng F. Hoffmann 頭 pái thê-chhut pò-kò. 到 1814 nî Eng-kok 伊-seng G. de Maton khak-jīn 彼 sī chi̍t 个 to̍k-li̍p 个 chèng-頭 (disease entity). 毋-chiah kiò-chò Tek-kok-phia̍h. Chit 个 chèng-頭 tōa-pō͘-份 無 siáⁿ-mih iàu-緊. 毋-kú 若-sī ū-sin 个 hū-jîn-人 佇 頭-chêng 3 kò goe̍h 敢-jiám-著 Tek-kok-phia̍h, pak-tó͘ 內 个 gín-仔 會 tài-著 鉎-thian-sèng Tek-kok-phia̍h chèng-hāu-kûn (congenital rubella syndrome). Gín-仔 chhut-世 會 ū 細-lia̍p 頭-náu, 目-chiu būn-tê, 鉎-thian-sèng sim-chōng-爸ⁿ, thé-tāng 無 到, koaⁿ, pî, hīⁿ-仔, phôe-撫... chiok chē 下-捅 攏 ū khoat-hām! 上 chió 33% 个 chit khoán gín-仔 chhut-世 chiū 敆 咱 講 bye-bye!

Hiang-káng-跤 sī án-chóaⁿ lâi--个?  毋 chai siáⁿ-mih sî-chūn, Eng-kok-人 lâi Hiang-káng 無 sûi 落 chûn, 閣 lâu 佇 chûn-chhng. 彼-chūn thiⁿ-氣 chin joa̍h. Chûn-chhng 內 joa̍h 閣 翕. Sip-就͘ chiok koân. Eng-kok-人 chhēng tn̂g-講-个, 跤 pau 到 密-chiuh-chiuh. Kòe 無 gōa kú, ū 人 phīⁿ-著 chhàu-跤-液, 閣 hoat-現 跤-貯 seⁿ chúi-phā, phòa-phôe, 閣 chin chiūⁿ! Eng-kok 伊-seng khòaⁿ 毋 捌 chit khoán jia̍t-tài chiah ū 个 chèng-頭. Chiū 共 hō-chò “Hong Kong foot”. Āu--lâi chiah chai gôan-𪜶 sī “seⁿ-ko͘”. Hiang-káng 敆 Tâi-oân 相-仝 攏 sī 佇 阿-jia̍t-tài. Joa̍h-thiⁿ joa̍h 閣 sip. Chin chē 人 phôe-撫 攏 會 seⁿ-ko͘, chiū sī seⁿ-仙--啦! Tān-sī 一-ti̍t 到 1908 nî Hiang-káng-跤 chiah chèng-sek lia̍t-入 伊-ha̍k 个 加-cho̍k. Án-ne 講 咱 chiū chai, 若 欲 hō-chò Tâi-oân-跤 嘛 OK.

講 到 chia, Tiong-kok Hì-iām (TKHI) sī siáⁿ-mih chèng-頭? Chiū sī ùi Tiong-kok thoân--chhut-lâi 个 hì-iām. Ū 2 chióng pán-pún: TKHI 2003 敆 TKHI 2019!

咱 eng-kai kì-tî 閣 chin chheng-chhó: 2003 nî ū hoat-seng chi̍t khoán hì-iām, chiū sī SARS! Chit 个 SARS chhím 開-sí chiū sī ùi Tiong-hôa Jîn-bîn Kiōng-hô-kok Kńg-冬-séng 開-sí--个. Chit khoán chèng-頭 个 爸ⁿ-goân-thé 嘛 sī 爸ⁿ-to̍k, kiò-chò Koan-chōng 爸ⁿ-to̍k (冠狀病毒). Koan-chōng 爸ⁿ-to̍k sī chi̍t 个 tōa 加-cho̍k. 會 hō͘ chia̍h-leng-lūi 敆 chiáu-仔-lūi phòa-爸ⁿ. Chia̍h-leng-lūi chiū sī chhiūⁿ 人, káu, seng-seng, gû, bé, ti, hîm, thò͘-仔, niáu-chhí, kóe-chí-bâ, o͘-跤-hiuⁿ... téng-téng. Chit lūi 个 撞-bu̍t chhut-世 了 khò chia̍h 阿-bó 个 leng-chúi tōa-hàn. 毋-chiah kiò-chò chia̍h-leng-lūi 撞-bu̍t. Kin-kì kho-ha̍k gián-糾 TKHI 2003 个 爸ⁿ-to̍k 洘-lêng sī ùi siā-hiuⁿ-niau (covet) 仔-sī bi̍t-pô (捌) lâi--个! Chit lūi 个 iá-seng 撞-bu̍t chiàu 講 無 eng-kai 敆 咱 人 相 kau-chhap. 毋-kú TKL(Tiong-kok-人) chiok 愛 chia̍h iá-沬 (野味)! 無-lūn sī thiⁿ-téng poe--个, thô͘-跤 趖--个, chúi--ni̍h siû--个, hoân-若 掠 會 著, chiū 攏 cháu 入-氣 𪜶 个 ngó͘-chōng lio̍k-拊, giān-giān 袂 siau kiān! Án-ne hō͘ 爸ⁿ-to̍k ū 支-hōe ti̍t-chiap 敆 人 chiap-chhiok. Chóng-講, TKHI 2003 pán hō͘ choân 細-介 chhiau-kòe 8,000 人 tài 爸ⁿ, 774 个 sí--氣! 咱 Tâi-oân 嘛 sí 37 个 人. (chham-洘: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Severe_acute_respiratory_syndrome)

2012 个 MERS (Middle East Respiratory Syndrome, Tiong-冬 Ho͘-khip Chèng-hāu-kûn) 个 爸ⁿ-to̍k 嘛 sio̍k Koan-chōng 爸ⁿ-to̍k 个 加-cho̍k, sī ùi lo̍k-tô lâi--个!

Chit-媽, TKHI 2019 pán 閣 lâi--啊! Chit pái pí téng-piàn 閣-較 khióng-pò͘ ! 到-焦ⁿ í-keng sí 400 gōa 个 人! Tān-sī chit 个 chu-liāu sī Tiong-kok thê-kiong. 毋 chai 敢 ū 翼-chāi? Góa kò-jîn 个 kiàn-改 sī eng-kai ū kúi cha̍p pōe, hoān-細 kúi pah pōe!

1.Chit chiah 爸ⁿ-to̍k, 2019-nCoV, 嘛 sī sio̍k Koan-chōng 爸ⁿ-to̍k. Eng-kai ū chin chē sèng-chit 敆 2003 彼 chiah 相-仝.

2.Sí-雺-lu̍t 無 2003 遐 koân, piáu-sī 會 閣 thòaⁿ chin kú, chin khoah.

3.到 joa̍h--人, 爸ⁿ-to̍k 較 無 遐 oa̍h-thiàu, liû-hêng eng-kai 會 較 chhé-退.

4.Chhì-giām-sèng 个 佇-liâu io̍h-仔 若 ū-hāu, 敢-siā thiⁿ-kong-擘!

5.Chiong-kî-bóe 猶-sī 愛 hoat-tián e̍k-biâu chò 奕-hông-siā, chhiūⁿ 咱 chù liû-敢 奕-hông-siā, 1 nî chù 1 pái.
