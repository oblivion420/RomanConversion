Tai5-uan5 Lo5-ma2-ji7 Hiap8-hue7 pan7-li2 “ Liam7 kua1-si1 , tsiah8 jiu5-hi5-muai5 ” ki3-liam7 uah8-tang7 i2-king1 10 kui2 tang1 . Kin1-ni5 in1-ui7 Tiong1-kok4 Bu2-han3 Hi3-iam7 iah8-tsing5 e5 ing2-hiong2 , tsuan5 Tai5-uan5 tsiann5 tse7 uah8-tang7 thing5-pan7 , a2-si7 ian5-au7 . Put4-ji5-ko3 , lan2 e5 uah8-tang7 te7-tiam2 an1-pai5 ti7 sik4-gua7 khong3-khuah4 e5 soo2-tsai7 , tsiu7 tsiau3-siong5 ki2-pan7 . 2 tiam2 28 lan2 tsun2-si5 tsun2-hun1 khai1-si2 . Tue3 kok4-kua1 “ Tai5-uan5 Tshui3-tshinn1 ” im1-gak8 , lan2 tua7-siann1 tshiunn3-tshut4 Tai5-uan5-lang5 e5 sim1-siann1 . Tsiap4-sua3 , lan2 ka7 1 ki1 1 ki1 pik4-hap8 tauh8-tauh8-a2 , ho2-le2-a2 hian3 hoo7 ui7 Tai5-uan5 hi1-sing1 e5 tsing1-ing1 . Te2-te2 1 hun1-tsing1 ai1-su1 , siau3-liam7 siu7-lan7-tsia2 , piau2-tat8 lan2 tui3 in1 e5 king3-i3 . Li2-su7-tiunn2 ti3-su5 e5 si5 piau2-si7 hian7-tshu2-si5 kuann1-hong1 e5 ki3-liam7 kan1-tann1 gi5-sik4 na7-tiann7 . Ki3-liam7 m7-na7 gi5-sik4 nia5 , lan2 pan7 Si1-kua1 Bun5-hua3-tseh4 e5 i3-gi7 u7 2 hang7 . Te7 1 hang7 , thau3-kue3 liam7 kua1-si1 siau3-liam7 siu7-lan7-tsia2 . Te7 2 hang7 , ng3-bang7 tsiann5-tso3 bin5-kan1 tseh4-jit8 , hoo7 au7-tai7 kiann2-sun1 liau2-kai2 tsia1-e5 lang5 e5 koo3-su7 , tai7-tai7 thuan5-tshiunn3 , lan2 e5 sian9-pai2 tsiah4 be7 peh8-peh8 hi1-sing1 . Hiap8-pan7 tan1-ui7 Tai5-uan5 Lam5-sia7 sia7-tiunn2 , Tai5-lam5-tshi7 kap4 Ko1-hiong5-tshi7 e5 tshi7-gi7-uan5 kiong7-tong5 hiang2-ing3 tsit4 e5 uah8-tang7 . Tai5-lam5-tshi7 Bun5-hua3-kiok8 ma7 tsin1 iau3-i3 . Hu3-kiok8-tiunn2 tshut4-sik8 kong2-tioh8 Bun5-hua3-kiok8 tsin3-hing5 228 bun5-hian3 kap4 khau2-sut8 lik8-su2 tiau5-tsa1 , tshut4-pan2 tshiu2-tsheh4 , siau7-kai3 27 e5 kap4 jin5-kuan5 lik8-su2 siong1-kuan1 e5 soo2-tsai7 . Hi1-bang7 hoo7 au7-tai7 liau2-kai2 lik8-su2 , tin1-sioh4 sian9-pai2 iong7 senn3-mia7 tsing1-tshu2--lai5 e5 tsu7-iu5 . Tsit4 kai2 lan2 siu1-tioh8 12 siu2 si1 , tsok4-tsia2 pau1-kuat4 Tai5-lo5-hue7 hue7-uan5 , kap4 Tai5-uan5-hong1 Kua1-gak8-tsip8 sing5-uan5 . Tai5-bun5 pit4-hue7 Li2-su7-tiunn2 Tan5 Bing5-jin5 , Tai5-uan5 Hu2-siann5 Ku7-tsheh4-tiam3 hu7-tsik4-jin5 ma7 hian3 si1 hoo7 sian9-pai2 . Tai5-uan5-hong1 Kua1-gak8-tsip8 sing5-uan5 tak8-ko3-gueh8 tsu7-hue7 tshong3-tsok4 Tai5-gi2-kua1 . Tan5 Tin1-gi5 kap4 Tsng1 Ge7-kuan1 tsit4 tui3 siau3-lian5 ang1-a2-boo2 te7 2 kai2 tsham1-ka1 ki3-liam7 uah8-tang7 . Ge7-kuan1 “ A1-pah4 e5 Thiann3 ” tsit4 siu2 si1 , iu5 Tin1-gi5 ian2-tshiunn3 . In1 hi1-bang7 hoo7 koh4-khah4 tse7 lang5 lai5 tsham1-u2 , soo2-pai2 iau1-tshiann2 Tai5-uan5-hong1 sing5-uan5 kiong7-tong5 phoo2-khik4 hian3 hoo7 sian9-pai2 . Tsit4 siu2 si1 biau5-sia2 Loo7-tik4 thau5 1 e5 khi2-kho1-i1 Ng5 Un1-kiong1 e5 koo3-su7 . Peh8-sik4 khiong2-poo3 si5-ki5 Ng5 i1-sing1 ti7 puann3-me5 hong5 liah8--khi3 . I1 e5 ui5-su1 an2-ne1 sia2 “ Gua2 e5 si2-si1 m7-thang1 lai5 nia2 . Gua2 hi1-bang7 kia3-hu3 hoo7 i1-hak8-inn7 , hoo7 i1-hak8-sing1 kai2-pho3 , tsing1-ka1 i1-hak8 ti3-sik4 ” . Ing2-ni5 lan2 ti7 hian7-tiunn5 tshuan5 jiu5-hi5-muai5 hoo7 tsham1-ka1-tsia2 . Kin1-ni5 in1-ing3 Tiong1-kok4 Hi3-iam7 iah8-tsing5 iong7 228 jun7-ko1 tshu2-tai7 jiu5-hi5-muai5 .
