Tsit4 kui2 ni5 tiann7-tiann7 kah4阮劇團hap8-tsok4 , “馬克白” , “熱天酣眠” , “城市戀歌進行曲” kap4 “嫁妝一牛車” , gua2 long2 u7 tsham1-ka1 , m7-koh4 it4-tit8 kam2-kak4 m7 tsai1-iann2 beh4 an2-tsuann2 ka7 Tai5-gi2 tsing3-khak4 e5 huat4-im1 ki3--loh8-lai5 .

Siong7-kho3 tsin3-tsing5 , pun2-lai5 si7 ui7-tioh8 khang1-khue3 su1-iau3 tsiah4 lai5--e5 . Siunn7-be7-kau3 siong7-kho3 liau2-au7 , tui3 gi2-gian5 e5 ian2-pian3 , huat4-im1 e5 se3-tsiat4 , kah4 lik8-su2 e5 ing2-hiong2 , long2 u7 khah4 tshim1-jip8 e5 liau2-kai2 .

Tsit4-ma2 i2-king1 sio2-khua2-a2 oh8-tioh8 Tai5-gi2 Lo2-ma2-ji7 e5 iong7-huat4 , hi1-bang7 ka1-ki7 e7-tang3 khai1-si2 thak8 Tai5-gi2 e5 tsok4-phin2 , hun3-lian7 ka1-ki7 thak8-sia2 e5 ling5-lik8 , it4-ting7 e7 tui3 Tai5-gi2 u7 koh4-khah4 tshim1 e5 liau2-kai2 . Ma7 hi1-bang7 ka1-ki7 pian3-sing5 e7-tang3 iong7 Tai5-gi2 tshong3-tsok4 e5 lang5 , ka7 lan2 e5 bo2-gi2 Tai5-gi2 e5 sui2 , u7 hoo7 koh4-khah4 tse7 lang5 sik8-sai7 e5 ki1-hue7 , sim7-tsi3 khai1-si2 ka1-jip8 Tai5-gi2 e5 tshong3-tsok4 .
