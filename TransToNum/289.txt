Tan5 Giok8-thiam1 kau3-siu7 kai2-sueh4 , lam5-hng1 tsit4-e5 soo2-tsai7 khah4 sio1-juah8 , tsit4-e5 tseh4-jit8 tioh8-si7 ui7-tioh8 beh4 ki3-liam7 tsit8-ni5 lai7-te2 siong7-kai3 juah8 e5 si5-kan1-tiam2 . Koo2-tsa2 si5 Uat8-lam5-gi2 lai7-te2 e5 " thang2 Một4 " ( ji7-bin7 siong7 si7 " it4 " gueh8 ) si7 ki2 ku7-lik8 e5 11 gueh8 , in1-ui7 hit4 si5 iong7 11 gueh8 tso3 ni5-tshe1 . Mui2 1 ko2 gueh8 e5 tshe1 2 tioh8 si7 gueh8-tshe1 , kio3 tso3 " thang2 lui1 , ngay3 tiến1 " ( the3-gueh8 , tsin3-gueh8 ) . Kan1-tann1 iong7 ui3 11 gueh8 khai1-si2 e5 tsit4-tsiong2 lik8-huat4 , 5 gueh8 tshe1 5 Tuan1-ngoo2-tseh4 tsiah4 u7 tsing3-khak4 e5 i3-gi7 , tioh8-si7 ki2 ni5-hun7 tsiann3-tiong1 e5 hit4 tsit8-kang1 ( siong7 kai3 juah8 e5 jit8-tsi3 ) . In1-ui7 tsit4-e5 si5-kan1 e5 thinn1-khi3 siong7-kai3 sio1-juah8 , tsin1 kan2-tan1 tioh8 e7 phua3-penn7 tso7-sing5 thuan5-jiam2-penn7 sio1 ue3 , Uat8-lam5-lang5 koh4 ka7 5 gueh8 tshe1 5 kio3-tso3 thau7-thang5-tseh4 . Ti7 5 gueh8 tshe1 5 tsit4 tsit8-kang1 , Uat8-lam5-lang5 thau3-tsa2 lim1 tsut8-bi2-tsiu2 , tsiah8 sng1-ko2 lai5 ka7 sin1-the2 lai7 e5 kia3-sing1-thang5 thai5--si2 . An2-ne1 i2-gua7 , tsit4 tsit8-kang1 Uat8-lam5-lang5 koh4 e7 iong7 ioh8-hioh8 buah4 ti7 tsing2-kah4 lai5 po2-hoo7 kap4 bi2-hua3 tsing2-kah4 , ping7-tshiann2 ti7 Tuan1-ngoo2 thau3-tiong1-tau3 iunn5-khi3 siong7 iang7 e5 si5-tsun7 khi3 ban2 ioh8-hioh8 , jian5-au7 tsiong1 ioh8-hioh8 phak8 ta1 tso3 bi7-lai5 tsit8-ni5 beh4 iong7 e5 ioh8-a2 . Tong1-tai7 Uat8-lam5-lang5 kue3 Tuan1-ngoo2 e5 khi3-hun1 lu2 lai5 lu2 poh8 , bo5-tshiunn7 Tai5-uan5 iah8-si7 Tsi1-na2 . Lam5-poo7 Hua5-jin5 koh4 u7 i5-tshi5 Tsi1-na2-sik4 e5 Tuan1-ngoo2-tseh4 hong1-siok8 sip8-kuan3 . In1-ui7 Uat8-lam5 Hua5-jin5 kan1-tann1 tsiam3 tsuan5-kok4 jin5-khau2 e5 1 % , tsit4-e5 hong1-siok8 sip8-kuan3 tui3 Uat8-lam5 tsuan5-kok4 lai5 kong2 ing2-hiong2 tsin1 iu2-han7 . Su7-sit8 siong7 , mui2 tsit8-e5 tui3 Tuan1-ngoo2-tseh4 e5 thuan5-suat4 , si7-m7-si7 ai3 tsiah8 tsang3 kap4 ko3 liong5-tsun5 e5 kuan3-si3 long2 bo5-kang5-khuan2 . Tuan1-ngoo2-tseh4 tsiah8 tsang3 kan1-tann1 si7 tong1-tai7 Tsi1-na2-lang5 e5 sip8-kuan3 nia7 . Ka7 Tuan1-ngoo2-tseh4 tong3-tso3 si1-jin5-tseh4 , koh4-khah4 si7 1939 ni5 Tiong7-khing3 bun5-ge7-kai3 khong3-tik8 hue7-gi7 ting7-iong7 tsit4-kang1 lai5 ki3-liam7 Khut4-guan5 ( Kin1-ku3 Kau3-iok8-poo7 kok4-gi2 su5-tian2 e5 kai2-sueh4 ) , tsioh4 tse1 iong7 lai5 the5-sing1 Tsi1-na2 jin5-bin5 e5 ai3 kok4 i3-sik4 .
