Guan5-lai5 si7 17 se3-ki2 tiong1 Ho5-lan1-lang5 lai5 Tai5-uan5-kang2 ( tsit4-ma2 Tai5-kang1 soo2-tsai7 ) , ka7 tsai7-te7 penn5-poo1-tsok8 Tshiah4-kham3-sia7 ( Ho5-lan1-mia5 Tsaccam1-sia7 ) be2 tsit4 te3 te7 , ti7 hia1 kian3-lip8 tshi7-ke1 , siong1-hang5 , kap4 tsing3-tshan5 tsiong2-tsiong2 , kio3-tso3 Provincia1 ( hing5-tsing3-khu1 ‘ sing2 ’ ) . 1652 ni5 , Kueh4 Huai5-it4 Su7-kiann7 liau2-au7 , Ho5-lan1-lang5 ti7 hia1 khi2 tsit8 e5 siann5-po2 po2-hoo7 , to7 si7 Tshiah4-kham3-lau5 ( Ang5-mng5-lau5 ) e5 tsing5-sin1 . Ui3 tsia1 Ho5-lan1-lang5 huat4-tian2 Tai5-lam5 tshi7-ke1 , khong3-tse3 Tai5-lam5 .
