Ti7 Ta2-kau2 ( gua2 e5 koo3-hiong1 , ma7 si7 gua2 sing1-uah8 e5 soo2-tsai7 ) tsiu7 u7 1 e5 tsin1 u7 uah8-lik8 e5 Tai5-gi2 sia7-thuan5 , “ Ta2-kau2 Tai5-gi2 Khai1-kang2-sia7 ” . M7-na7 tak8-ko3-gueh8 u7 pan7 1 tiunn5 tsuan5 Tai5-gi2 kang2-ian2 , koh4 u7 2 pai2 Tai5-bun5 thak8-tsheh4-hue7 . Bue2-tshiu2 koh4 huat4-tian2 Tai5-gi2 gin2-a2-koo2 e5 hu7-siok8 tsoo1-tsit4 . Pue5-hun3 kong2 Tai5-gi2 gin2-a2-koo2 e5 lau7-su1 , ma7 tshong3-tsok4 kui2-na7 pun2 Tai5-gi2 ue7-pun2 . “ Ta2-kau2 Tai5-gi2 Khai1-kang2-sia7 ” si7 Ko1-hiong5 Te7-it4 Sia7-khu1 Tai7-hak8 e5 tsu7-tsu2-sing3 sia7-thuan5 . Tsi2-to7 lau7-su1 si7 tng5-ki5 ti7 te7-it4-suann3 phah4-piann3 kau3-hak8 e5 Tan5 Kim1-hua1 lau7-su1 . Ti7 thau5 tsit8 jim7 sia7-tiunn2 Lim5 Bun5-tsing1 e5 si5-ki5 gua2 tsiu7 na2 tsham1-ka1 , na2 khai1-si2 tau3-sann1-kang7 pan7 uah8-tang7 . Kau3 gua2 tsiap4 te7-ji7 jim7 e5 sia7-tiunn2 ( 2018 ~ 2020-ni5 ) , it4-tit8 u7 ka7 sia7-thuan5 e5 uah8-tang7 ng3 gua7 ( Sia7-tai7 i2-gua7 , ma7 si7 Tai5-gi2 khoo1-a2 i2-gua7 . ) ian5-sin1 e5 siunn7-huat4 kap4 tshi3-giam7 . In1-ui7 teh4 tshui1-sak4 Tai5-gi2 , Tai5-bun5 e5 kue3-thing5 tiong1-kan1 , ma7 san2-sing1 tse7-tse7 siunn7-huat4 kap4 the2-hue7 . Loo7-bue2 tsiah4 tsiann5-tso3 beh4 pan7 “ Tai5-bun5 Phok4-lam2-hue7 ” e5 siunn7-huat4 . Siunn7-beh4 tshi3-khuann3-mai7 kam2 thang1 kai2-kuat4 ping5-soo3-si5 teh4 tshui1-kong2 tu2--tioh8 e5 tsit8-kua2 khun3-king2 kap4 han7-tse3 . “ Tai5-bun5 Phok4-lam2-hue7 ” beh4 tshi3-giam7 e5 hong1-sik4 , si7 beh4 tsiann5-tso3 1 e5 sin1 se3-tai7 Tai5-bun5 uah8-tang7 e5 tian2-huan7 . An2-ne1 kong2 u7 khah4 tshau3-phui3 , m7-koh4 khak4-sit8 tsio2-tsio2 lang5 tsai1 thang1 an2-ne1 tso3 . Sim7-tsi3 pan7 liau2 tse7-tse7 khoo1-a2-lai7 e5 iu2-tsi3 ma7 m7 tsai1 gua2 teh4 pinn3-siann2-bang2 . Ing2-kue3 kau3 tann1 , Tai5-gi2 , Tai5-bun5 siong1-kuan1 e5 khui3-lat8 , khah4 tse7 si7 teh4 khi2 tshu3 e5 kin1-ki1 . Ui3 khoo1-a2-lai7 khi3 giu2 u7 kang7-khuan2 sin3-liam7 e5 lang5 lai5 tsham1-u2 , tso3 tse7-tse7 ki1-tshoo2 kang1-ting5 . M7-koh4 tsiu7 in1-ui7 an2-ne1 khah4 bo5 tsu3-tiong7 ui3 sia7-hue7 it4-puann1-tik4 e5 kak4-too7 lai5 su1-kho2 . Gua2 kue3-khi3 in1-ui7 u7 ti7 sin1-tshong3 thuan5-tui7 , sin1-tshong3 kong1-si1 tso3 san2-phin2 e5 king1-giam7 . Tui3 hok8-bu7 siat4-ke3 ( service1 design1 ) , ah8-si7 siat4-ke3 su1-i5 ( design1 thinking1 ) e5 khai3-liam7 u7 tsit8-kua2 jin7-bat4 . Kan2-tan1 kong2 tsiu7 si7 beh4 an2-na2 siat4-ke3 ho2 e5 liu5-thing5 kap4 the2-giam7 lai5 pang1-tsan7 lan2 in2-tshua7 lan2 soo2 ng3-bang7 e5 tui3-siong7 , sun7 lan2 soo2 siunn7 lai5 kau3 lan2 e5 bok8-phiau1 . Tsiu7 si7 ui3 liau2-kai2 , jin7-tong5 , kau3 sit8-tsian7 . U7 oh8-kue3 hing5-siau1 e5 lang5 tsiu7 tsai1 “ hing5-siau1 lau7-a2 ” e5 khai3-liam7 . An2-na2 tsit8 ian5 tsit8 ian5 ka7 lang5-kheh4 in2-tshua7--jip8-lai5 . Siong7 iau3-kin2--e5 tsiu7 si7 khi3 kai2-kuat4 hit4 e5 lau7-a2 si7 that4 ti7 to2-ui7 ( bottleneck4 ) . Pan7 1 e5 sia7-hue7-tik4 u7 pan7 u7 giang1 koh4 tsin1 khiang1 e5 Tai5-gi2 , Tai5-bun5 uah8-tang7 , thang1 lai5 khip4-in2 tshing1-siau3-lian5 ping5-iu2 tsu3-i3 , tsham1-u2 . Sim7-tsi3 tsiann5-tso3 si5-kiann5 e5 in3-siong7 , tsiu7 si7 “ Tai5-bun5 Phok4-lam2-hue7 ” e5 bok8-tik4 . M7 si7 beh4 pan7 lai5 hoo7 khoo1-a2-lai7 e5 lang5 ka1-ti7 tsham1-ka1 , ka1-ti7 thiong3--e5 . Ma7 in1-ui7 bok8-phiau1 tsin1 bing5-khak4 , khui3-lat8 ma7 iu2-han7 , soo2-pai2 gua2 liah8 kui2 e5 tiong7-tiam2 lai5 tso3 . Thau5 1 tsan5 , ing2-hiong2 u7 ing2-hiong2-lat8 e5 lang5 . Ma7 si7 bi7-lai5 gian5-gi2 beh4 thuan5-sing5 siong7 iau3-kin2 e5 tsok8-kun5 , tshing1-siau3-lian5 ping5-iu2 . Tsha1-put4-to1 tai7-hak8 beh4 pit4-giap8 kau3 tu2 kiat4-hun1 , u7 se3-han3 gin2-a2 e5 ka1-tiunn2 . Liong7-ki5-iok4 si7 20 ~ 45-hue3 . Tsit4 tin7 lang5 siong7 u7 ki1-hue7 tsiann5-tso3 koh4-khah4 tua7 e5 khui3-lat8 lai5 pang1-tsan7 Tai5-gi2 , Tai5-bun5 huat4-tian2 , thng5-thuann3 lan2 ka1-ti7 e5 gi2-bun5 . In1-ui7 in1 ti7 sia7-hue7--nih8 , ka1-ting5--nih8 siong7 u7 ing2-hiong2-lat8 , ma7 siong7 uah8-thiau3 . Te7-ji7 tsan5 , tsiu7 si7 beh4 pan7 siann2-mih4 uah8-tang7 , an2-na2 pan7 . U7 siann2 uah8-tang7 tse1 khah4 ku7-the2 , gua2 sing2--khi2-lai5 bo5 koh4 kong2 . Hian7-tiunn5 e5 uah8-tang7 u7 khah4 tse7 me5-kak4 ai3 tshu2-li2 , iu5-ki5 beh4 ing5-tso7 1 e5 hong5 khi2-moo1-giang1 , tsuan5 Tai5-gi2 , koh4 e7 tsu7-jian5 tsiap4-tshiok4 Tai5-bun5 e5 khuan5-king2 . Thiah4-peh8 kong2 , tsin1 oh4 . Kui1-e5 uah8-tang7 e5 siat4-ke3 tsiu7 si7 kuan1-kian7 . Iong7 tsit8-kua2 ku7-the2 e5 le7 lai5 suat4-bing5 . Phi3-ju5 kong2 , gun2 e5 tsi3-kang1 uan5-tsuan5 iau1-kiu5 ai3 u7 tsuan5 Tai5-gi2 e5 ling5-lik8 , koh4 ai3 si7 siau3-lian5-ke1 . Phah4-phua3 Tai5-gi2 si7 koo2-tsa2-lang5 tsiah4 kong2 , siau3-lian5-lang5 long2 be7-hiau2 e5 khik4-pan2 in3-siong7 . Pan7 siau3-lian5-khui3 e5 Tai5-gi2 tshi7-a2 , iau1-kiu5 ai3 u7 siunn7-beh4 kiong7-tong5 tshong3-tso7 kong2 Tai5-gi2 e5 khuan5-king2 tsiah4 thang1 lai5 tsham1-ka1 . Hian7-tiunn5 tsiu7 u7 tsiok4 tse7 siau3-lian5-lang5 lai5 pai5-tann3 . M7-na7 u7 Tai5-bun5 phin2-pai5 e5 tiam3 , koh4 u7 be7 tsio2 Tai5-bun5 e5 sin1 san2-phin2 lai5 hian7-tiunn5 kap4 lang5-kheh4 kau1-liu5 hoo7-tong7 . Ki5-tiong1 koh4 u7 tann3-a2 e5 thau5-ke1 ka1-ti7 koh4 ti7 tann3-a2 thau5-tsing5 hoo7 in1 tau1 e5 gin2-a2 tshua7 Tai5-gi2 uah8-tang7 . Tse1 si7 gian5-gi2 e5 poo7-hun7 .
