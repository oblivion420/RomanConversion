台文通訊BONG1報: Tshi7-liau7 Sio1-uann7 Tsiah8 e5 Niau1 kap4 Kau2 飼料sio1換食e5貓kap4狗
【 Lah4-jih4 Gu7-gian5 Kong2-koo2 】

作者：：蕭平治

Niau1-a2 kap4 kau2-a2 si7 lang5 tshu3-lai7 e5 ho2-ping5-iu2 , niau1-a2 ka7 niau2-tshi2 , kau2-a2 koò1 mng5-tiann5 , hun1-kang1 hap8-tsok4 u7 tsik4-jim7 , bok8-kuai3 tsu2-lang5 sioh4-mia7-mia7 . Tak8 sann1-tng3 niau1-a2 long2 u7 hi5 thang1 tshng2 , kau2-a2 long2 u7 bah4-kut4 thang1 ge3 , u7-kau3 ho2 kau2-mia7 ho2 niau1-mia7 .

M7 tsai1 si7 kenn1-tng3 tsiah8 liau2 siunn1-kue3 ho2 , ah8-si7 sim1-lai7 khi2 uan3-toò1 , niau1-a2 kau2-a2 suah4 it4-tit8 leh4 tsim1-tsiok4 tui3-hong1 e5 tsiah8-mih8 , he1 bah4-kut4 kai3 ho2-tsiah8 e5 khuan2-sik4 ? he1 hi5-a2 khuann3--khi2-lai5 tsiok4 ho2-tsiah8-khuan2 ?

U7 tsit8 kang1 tsu2-lang5 sang3 kenn1-tng3 lai5 , niau1-a2 kau2-a2 khai1-si2 siong3 tui3-hong1 tsiah8-mih8 , tan2 tsu2-lang5 li7-khui1 , in1 suah4 hiong5-hiong5 tang5-tse5 khui1-tshui3 : Lan2 lai5 sio1-uann7 tsiah8 ho2--bo5 ?

“ Ho2--oo7 ! ”

“ Ngh4 ! Hi5-a2 na2-e7 tsiah4-ni7 kau7-tshi3 ? ”

“ Ah4 he1 bah4-kut4 na2-e7 tsiah4-ni7 ting7 , ting7-khok4-khok4 si7 bueh4 an2-tsuann2 tsiah8 ? ”

Bo5-gua7-ku2 e5 si5-kan1 , niau1-a2 iaunn1-iaunn1-kio3 , “ Ah4 ah4 gua2 e5 tshui3-khi2--lah4 , aih4-ah8-ue9 ! Gua2 e5 tshui3-khi2--lah4 ! ”

Kau2-a2 ma7 kainn1-kainn1-kio3 , “ Gua2 e5 ge5-tso5-huann7--lah4 , gua2 e5 ge5-tso5-huann7 thiann3-tiuh4-tiuh4 , tshak8 tioh8 tshi3--lah4 , au7-pai2 gua2 m7 kann2--ah4--lah4 ! ”

Niau1-a2 kau2-a2 ai1-pe7-kio3-bo2 e5 tshi1-tsham2 hau2-siann1 khi3 hoo7 tsu2-lang5 thiann1--tioh8 .

“ Lin2--oo3 ! U7-iann2 gong7 kah4 be7 pe5-tsiunn7 , khiam1-kai1--e5 ! ”

Gu7-i3 : M7-thang1 huai5-gi5 , m7-thang1 tshi3-tham3 . Huai5-gi5 , tshi3-tham3 , si7 bo5 sin3-jim7 e5 khai1-si2 .
