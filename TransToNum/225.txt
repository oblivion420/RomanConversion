Bo2-gi2 si7 Tai5-gi2 e5 lang5 , na7 u7 Tai5-gi2 phainn2 oh8 e5 kam2-kak4 , piau2-si7 i1 i2-king1 tann7-sit4 gi2-gian5 ng5-kim1-ki5 tsin1 ku2--ah4 .

Ti7 tsing3-siong5 tsong7-thai3 , siong7 kan1-tan1 oh8 e5 gi2-gian5 si7 ka1-ti7 e5 bo2-gi2 . Gin2-a2 tshut4-si3 , soo2-u7 e5 gi2-im1 long2 e7-hiau2 . Soo2-i2 tsi2-iau3 u7 lang5 kong2 , i1 kuan3-si3 thiann1 kap4 tue3 liam7 , put4-ti1 put4-kak4 to7 e7-hiau2 , bo5 soo2-ui7 khun3-lan5 e5 soo2-tsai7 . Tann7-sit4 ng5-kim1-ki5 ( 7 hue3 i2-tsing5 ) , oh8 bo2-gi2 suah4 na2-tshiunn7 teh4 oh8 gua7-gi2 . Tong1-jian5 kam2-kak4 khun3-lan5 . Tsit4 khuan2 khun3-lan5 , e7 tue3 ni5-ki2 e5 tsing1-ka1 lai5 tsing1-ka1 . Soo2-i2 na7 beh4 poo2-kiu3 , ma7 si7 ju2 kin2 ju2 ho2 .

Gi2-gian5 hak8-ka1 bat4 an2-ne1 hing5-iong5 , ti7 gi2-gian5 ng5-kim1-ki5 , oh8 gi2-gian5 na2-tshiunn7 iong7 khioh4--e5 . Soo2-i2 tsi2-iau3 u7 khuan5-king2 , m7-tann1 bo2-gi2 , gin2-a2 tsiap4-tshiok4--tioh8 e5 gi2-gian5 long2 oh8 e7 khi2--lai5 . Gin2-a2 tu2 tshut4-si3 , soo2-u7 e5 gi2-im1 long2 e7-hiau2 . Na7 tann7-sit4 ng5-kim1-ki5 , bo5 iong7--tioh8 e5 gi2-im1 to7 na2-tshiunn7 tan3 khi3 pun3-so3-thang2 . Tsha1-put4-to1 sio2-hak8 tiong1-ko1 ni5-kip4 , na7 u7 siunn7-beh4 ka7 khioh4--tng2-lai5 , si7 ai3 ke1 khai1 tsing1-sin5 khi3 pun3-so3-thang2 tshue7 nia5 , iau2 u7 ki1-hue7 khioh4--tng2-lai5 . I2-au7 pun3-so3 na2 tsik4 na2 tse7 , ai3 piann3 pun3-so3-tshia1 , sim7-tsi3 to3 pun3-so3-tiunn5 . Beh4 koh4 ka7 tshue7--tng2-lai5 ju2 lai5 ju2 khun3-lan5 . Na7 kue3 60 hue3 , huan7-se3 to7 sio1-liau2--khi3--ah4 .

Ti7 u7 gi2-gian5 khuan5-king2 e5 soo2-tsai7 , si3-ke3 long2 u7 lang5 kap4 li2 kong2 , na2-tshiunn7 tak8-ke1 teh4 ka3--li2 . Koh4 si7 ti7 sik8-sai7 e5 khuan5-king2 oh8--tioh8 . Gi2-im1 m7-na7 tsun2-khak4 , ma7 bo5 pue7 sin1-gi2 , ki3 bun5-huat4 e5 hu7-tam1 . Tse1 to7 si7 an2-tsuann2 gua7-kok4 siau3-lian5-gin2-a2 kau3 tsit8 e5 soo2-tsai7 , na7 u7 khah4 ku2 e5 thing5-liu5 , u7 hiah4 tse7 bian2-tsinn5 e5 lau7-su1 , tua7-poo7-hun7 long2 e7 oh8 tong1-te7 e5 gi2-gian5 , be7 ke3-kau3 oh8 he1 u7 loo7-ing7--bo5 .

Kho2-sioh4 lan2 e5 tsing3-hu2 kap4 ka1-tiunn2 bo5 i3-sik4-tioh8 tsit4 e5 iu1-se3 . Tian1-to3 kiann1 bo2-gi2 ing2-hiong2 Hua5-gi2 e5 hak8-sip8 , king3-jian5 hong3-khi3 bo2-gi2 . Tai5-uan5-gin2-a2 hong3-khi3 bo2-gi2 e5 kiat4-ko2 , gi2-im1 pian3 eh8 . In1 koh4 bo5 Ing1-gi2 khuan5-king2 , oh8 Ing1-gi2 ke1 tsin1 tsiah8-lat8 . Soo2-i2 han5-ban7--e5 ju2 oh8 ju2 bo5 sin3-sim1 , gau5--e5 suah4 pian3 kiau1-ngoo7 . Kiat4-ko2 si7 Ing1-gi2 pian3 kho2-tshi3 kap4 pi2 sing5-tsik4 iong7 nia5 . Khah4 tsio2 lang5 e7 tsu7-tong7 be2 kho3-gua7 e5 Ing1-bun5-tsheh4 lai5 khuann3 . Ping5-si5 ma7 tsio2 thiann1 gin2-a2 kong2 Ing1-gi2 .

Ka1-tiunn2 na7 tsai1-iann2 sian7-iong7 gi2-gian5 ng5-kim1-ki5 , li7-iong7 gin2-a2 gi2-gian5 ling5-lik8 tng1 ho2 e5 si5 , sing1 khioh4-tioh8 bo2-gi2 . Na7 koh4 u7 Kheh4-gi2 , Ing1-gi2 , Jit8-gi2 , Uat8-gi2 ting2 ki5-thann1 gi2-gian5 khuan5-king2 , ma7 e7-sai2 sun7-sua3 khioh4 , tso3-hue2 ka7 oh8--khi2-lai5 .
