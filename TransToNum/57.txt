Lan2 u7 tsit4 e5 Jit8-thau5-hue1 hng5-a2 ai3 kam2-sia7--li2 , thau5 tsit8 pai2 lai5 , to7 ai3-tioh8 tsit4 e5 hng5-a2 ! Hng5-a2 tai7-iok4 4 hun1 , khia7-ke1 uah8-tong7 khu1 kap4 tsing3-tsoh4 khu1 , kok4 tsiam3 tsit8-puann3 . Ping5-si5 siong7-pan1 ka3 Tai5-gi2 , khah4 tsio2 u7 si5-kan1 thang1 tua3 ti7 tsia1 , tsit4-ma2 tu2-tioh8 Bu2-han3 Hi3-iam7 , tsin7-liong7 mai3 tshut4-mng5 , suah4 u7 kui2-kang1-a2 ka3-jit8 thang1 lai5 tsia1 tua3 , kam2-kak4 tsiok4 kam2-un1 . Kam2-un1 e7-tang3 ka7 iu1-sim1 hua3-tso3 un2-sim1 , ti7 tsia1 too7-kue3 Bu2-han3 Hi3-iam7 lai5 jiau2-luan7 e5 tshun1-thinn1 . Hiann5-hue2 si7 kuann5-thinn1--lang5 siong7 un1-luan2 e5 tai7-tsi3 . Hue2 tiam2 toh8 liau2-au7 , ka7 tua7-khoo1 tsha5 lok4 ti7 hue2-loo5 lai7-te2 , hoo7 un5-un5-a2 sio1 , m7-koh4 ai3 tsim1-tsiok4 i5-tshi5 tsit4 pha1 hue2 mai3 hua1--khi3 . Tse7 ti7 hue2-loo5-a2 pinn1 phah4 tian7-nau2 , kam2-kak4 tsiok4 un1-luan2 . Thiann1-tioh8 tsiau2-a2 siann1 , hong1 tshue1 tshiu7-hioh8-a2 e5 siann1 , sam1-put4-goo7-si5-a2 thiann1-tioh8 ke1 thi5 , kau2 pui7 . U7-si5 koh4 thiann1-tioh8 niau2-tshi2 ti7 thian1-pong5 tsau2-sio1-jiok4 e5 siann1 . E7-tang3 an1-jian5 tsu7-tsai7 ti7 hng5-a2 sing1-uah8 , sit8-tsai7 tsiok4 u7 hok4-khi3--lah4 . Hiann5-hue2 , hoo7 gua7 siunn7-khi2 tso3 Tai5-gi2 e5 sim1-tsing5 , beh4 i5-tshi5 tsit4 pha1 hue2 mai3 hua1--khi3 , ma7 ai3 u7 tsit8-tiam2-a2 kang1-hu1 . Mi1-tsih4-luh4 sang3 ! E7-ki3-tit4 lan2 tu2 sik8-sai7 e5 si5 , to7 u7 iok4-sok4 beh4 tso3 Tai5-gi2 tsi3-kang1 , li2 kong2 “ Lan2 mai3 hi1-hua1 , ai3 tshiunn7 ting7-tsha5 an2-ne1 tsiah4 sio1 e7 ku2 . ” Tsit4 ku3 ue7 hoo7 gua2 tsiok4 kam2-tong7 . Au7--lai5 li2 tsin1-tsiann3 ham7 gua2 thin7-thau5 tso3 . Li2 tsong2--si7 kong2 “ Si5-se3 tsiah4-ni7 bai2 , lan2 tso3 e5 su7-kang1 to7-si7 koo3 tsit4 pha1 hue2 mai3 hua1--khi3 , thing3-hau7 gau5 e5 lang5 koh4 lai5 ka7 hue2 hun3 hoo7 khah4 tua7 pha1--leh4 ” . Bo5 m7-tioh8 , ai3 iong7-sim1 , Tai5-gi2 hue2 tsiah4 koo3 e7 ho2-se3 , gua2 to7-si7 khioh4 tsha5 king2 tsha5 iat8-hong1 tiam2-hue2 e5 lang5 . Gua2 ai3 tsiok4 liau2-kai2 bo5-kang5 tsha5-tsit4 ing1-kai1 ai3 iong7 ti7 siann2-mih4 bo5-kang5 e5 si5-ki1 , tak8 te3 tsha5 long2 u7 loo7-iong7--tioh8 . Sing1 lai5 kong2 khi2-hue1 . Tsit8 khai1-si2 su1-iau3 tsit8 tiunn1 ku7 po3-tsua2 , iong7 laih4-tah4 khiat4 hoo7 toh8-hue2 , khin1-khin1-a2 ka7 he7 ti7 loo5-a2 te2 , tsiah4 thinn1 tsit8-tui1-a2 iu3-tsha5-a2-ki1 , iah4-si7 phann3-tsha5 e7 khah4 ho2 hiann5 . Tan2 hue2 un5--khi2-lai5 to7 thinn1 tiong1-pan7 e5 tsha5-a2-ki1 , u7-si5-a2 tu2-tioh8 hue2 bo5-kau3 iam7 , gua2 e7 in1 tsit8 pe2 tsha5-in1 ka7 tsinn1--jip8-khi3 , khak4-ting7 hue2 ong7--khi2-lai5--ah4 , tsiah4 hiannh4 khah4 u7 tong3-thau5 e5 tua7-khoo1 tsha5 lai5 sio1 , na7-bo5 , hue2 liam1-mi1 to7 koh4 hua1--khi3--ah4 . Tse1 kue3-ting5 kap4 sio1 Tai5-gi2 hue2 kang5-khuan2 ； Gua2 khui1-pan1 ka3 Tai5-gi2 kap4 lang5 puann5-nua2 , si7 hit4 tiunn1 ku7 po3-tsua2 . Iong7 Tai5-uan5-ue7 tso3 laih4-tah4 khiat4 hoo7 toh8-hue2 . Siong7-kho3 ui3 bo5 Tai3-gi5 i3-sik4 , kong2 kau3 tshoo1-tshian2 liau2-kai2 Tai5-bun5 tshu2-king2 , sng3 si7 thinn1 tsit8-tui1-a2 iu3-ue1 e5 tsha5-a2-ki1 , lai5 tshi3 khuann3 e7 toh8--bo5 ? Tshua7 lang5 lai5 tsham1-ka1 Tai5-bun5 uah8-tong7 , hoo7 lang5 jin7-bat4 Tai5-bun5 se3-kai3 , to7-si7 in1 hit4 pe2 tsha5-in1 . Tsit4-tsun7 , hue2 khai1-si2 u7 koo3-ting7 e5 un1-too7--ah4 . Koh4 lai5 to7-si7 ai3 tso3 tua7 kuat4-ting7 suan2 loo7-suann3 , in1-ui7 beh4 kap4 lang5 sio1 king7 ma7 ai3 u7 sann1-nng7-poo7-tshit4--a3 ! It4-ting7 ai3 sing5-tsiu7 ka1-ti7 , to7 tshiunn7 hiannh4 u7 tong3-thau5 e5 tua7-khoo1 tsha5 lai5 sio;1 gua2 e7 koo2-le7 lang5 tsin3-siu1 , thak8 Tai5-bun5 gian2-kiu3-soo2 , thak8 Tai5-bun5 phok4-su7 , tsun2-pi7 tso3 Tai5-bun5 soo2 kau3-siu7 . U7 ki1-hue7 to7 in2-tshua7 gau5-lang5 kin1-sui5 Tai5-bun5 kau3-siu7 thau5-lang5 , kap4 in1 hak8-sip8 . Hi1-bang7 in1 u7 ki1-hue7 ua2-kin7 Tai5-gi2 thuan5-the2 na2 hok8-bu7 na2 huat4-hui1 ling5-lik8 lian7 kang1-hu1 . Tai5-gi2 hue2 su1-iau3 Tai5-gi2 lang5 kiong7-tong5 e5 jiat8-too7 sio1 king7 , gua2 ti7 tiam2-hue2 toh8-hue2 kap4 i5-tshi5 tsit4 pha1 hue2 mai3 hua1--khi3 e5 kue3-ting5 tiong1 , khuann3-kinn3 tak8 te3 tsha5 e5 loo7-iong7 .
