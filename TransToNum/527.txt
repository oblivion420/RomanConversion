Hian7-tshu2-si5 ing7 tsiann5 m7-kam1 e5 sim1-tsing5 teh4 sia2 tsit4 tiunn1 phue1 . Ti7 le2-pai3-ji7 am3-si5 bo5 khuann3-tioh8 li2 lai5 siong7-kho3 . Jian5-ki5-au7 tak8-ke1 thiann1-tioh8 li2 tui3 lau5-thui1 ting2-bin7 puah8--loh8-lai5 siu7-siong1 e5 siau1-sit4 . Kui1 am3 siong7-kho3 sim1 long2 bun7-bun7 be7 song2-khuai3 . Tak8-ke1 long2 ti7 sim1-lai7 thau1-thau1-a2 teh4 hau2 . In1-ui7 guan2 long2 m7 si7 bo5 hueh4 , bo5 bak8-sai2 e5 lang5 . Ma7 in1-ui7 li2 si7 tsit8 e5 ui7 Tsu2 tso3-kang1 koh4 tsin7-tsit4 e5 tiunn2-lo2 . Ia7 si7 guan2 tak8-ke1 sim1-bak8-tiong1 ing2-uan2 e5 ho2 pan1-tiunn2 . E7-ki3-tit4 ti7 tsiann5 tse7 ni5 i2-tsing5 , gua2 khi3 tsham1-ka1 Hai2-an1 Kau3-hue7 Ng5 Tian2 tiunn2-lo2 e5 ko3-piat8-sik4 . Sim1-tsing5 ma7 si7 tshiunn7 tsit4-ma2 an2-ni1 m7-kam1 . I1 si7 Hai2-an1 Kau3-hue7 khai1-ki1 guan5-tsoo2 e5 tiunn2-lo2 . Ma7 si7 tsiann5 jiat8-sim1 teh4 hok8-sai7 Tsu2 . U7 tsit8 kang1 , khia5 i1 sim1-ai3 e5 oo1-too2-bai2 tai7-piau2 Hai2-an1 Kau3-hue7 beh4 khi3 A1-lian1 Kau3-hue7 khui1-hue7 . Bo5-gi5-goo7 suah4 ti7 Khuan5-kiu5-loo7 huat4-sing1 su7-koo3 . I1 bo5 khia5 kau3 A1-lian1 , to7 an2-ni1 khia5 tui3 thian1-kok4 khi3 . Bong5 Tsu2 un1-tiau3 . Ko3-piat8-sik4 tsiann5 tse7 tshin1-iu2 tsham1-ka1 . Tak8-pai2 na7 siunn7-khi2 Ng5 Tian2 tiunn2-lo2 , sim1-lai7 to7 e7 siunn7 : Siong7-te3 na7-si7 mng7 i1 tui3 to2-ui7 lai5--e5 ? I1 to7 e7-tang3 ka7 Siong7-te3 kong2 , gua2 tui3 hok8-sai7 e5 puann3-tiong1-too5 lai5--e5 . Gua2 teh4 siunn7 , i1 e5 kiann2-kiann2 sun1-sun1 ma7 e7 the3 i1 kam2-kak4 tsiann5 kong1-ing5 . Guan2 thiann1-tioh8 li2 e5 thau5 kho3-siong1 , lau5 tsin1 tse7 hueh4 , thinn7 tsin1 tse7 tsiam1 . Tshiu2-kut4 koh4 tng7--khi3 . Tak8-ke1 bak8-sai2 kiong7-beh4 lin3--loh8-lai5 , ma7 long2 ti7 sim1-lai7 teh4 the3 li2 ki5-to2 , sui1-bong2 guan2 m7 tsai1 na2 e7 huat4-sing1 tsit4 khuan2 i3-gua7 . Tsong2--si7 gua2 teh4 siunn7 , Siong7-te3 tik4-khak4 u7 I1 bi2-ho2 e5 tsi2-i3 . Siok8-gi2 kong2 , “ phah4-tng7 tshiu2-kut4 tian1-to3 iong2 ” . Kiam2-tshai2 Siong7-te3 ai3 li2 sing1 hioh4-khun3--tsit8-tsam7-a2 . Tan2 li2 sin1-the2 khong1-hok8 e5 si5 , to7 e7-tang3 the3 Siong7-te3 tso3 koh4-khah4 tse7 e5 kang1 , hoo7 Siong7-te3 tua7-tua7 su2-iong7 . Guan5-pun2 siunn7-beh4 sia2 tsit8-kua2-a2 tshio3-khue1 hoo7 li2 tshio3-tshio3--leh4 . Hoo7 li2 e5 sim1-tsing5 khah4 ho2--leh4 . Tsong2--si7 kiann1 li2 siunn1 kik4-tong7 , khang1-tshui3 e7 thiann3 . Ka1-siong7 in3-pio2-ki1 e5 bak8-tsui2 tsiann5 kui3 , na7-si7 ing7 siunn1 tse7 , guan2 e5 gin2-a2 e7 tshue1-kau2-le5 . Soo2-i2 gua2 sia2 kau3 tsia1 to7 ho2 . Koh4-tsai3 kong2 , tsu7-koo2 ing1-hiong5 sin1-the2 hi1 , tioh8-ai3 khah4 po2-tiong7--leh4 .
